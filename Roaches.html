It is no surprise that cockroaches can and do live through many types of disasters because they are resilient. You will find them in abandoned homes, in the wild, and even in homes that are inhabited. They can make their nest anywhere and find food and water nearby to feast on.

German cockroaches are one species that commonly find their way into your home and they are the hardest to get rid of. Common or American cockroaches are easier to eradicate, but the importance in both situations is to seek out pest control services in NY.

Many cockroaches prefer to live on wood, which means that you will usually find them under your cabinets or in cardboard boxes. One of the unique things about cockroaches, especially German ones, is that their fecal matter contains a pheromone that emits a scent to other roaches and lets them know it is a good place to stay.
