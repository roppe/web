One of the hardest tasks in ant control is identification of the ant itself. Some treatments work well for specific species and others do not, so identification is vital for success.

There are more than 700 types of ants in the US and many of them are identified by the types of trails they leave and their overall habits. Ants do not usually travel as individuals and if you see one or two, there are many more somewhere else out of sight.

In addition, when ants are identified, our technicians will take note of what size they are, their color, and whether they move in a specific pattern or are attracted to a specific type of attractant whether water, food, etc.

Ant Control Service in New York

Ant control can be difficult, even when you do know the type of species the ant is, so it is recommended that you work with a professional who is licensed and experienced. Some ant species are able to be controlled using a bait-like substance while others need a different type of treatment method.
