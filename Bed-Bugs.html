Signs That You May Have Bed Bugs

You may be wondering just how you can tell that you have bed bugs in your home. They are sneaky and skilled hitchhikers who will find themselves a way into your home when possible. These little pests get their names because they choose to live in your mattress or nearby it.

Bed bugs themselves are able to hide from your eye and can be difficult to find, but they do leave behind evidence that they have been somewhere. You will be able to tell that you have bed bugs if you notice any of these signs:

  • Fecal stains on the bedding or your mattress
  • Exoskeletons, shells, or bug skin on your mattress
  • Blood stains on your clothes, sheets, or the mattress

One of the most common signs that bed bugs are present is the bed bug bite that may appear on your body. Some of the characteristics of a bed bug bite include:

  • Multiple bites or a straight line of bites on your body
  • Itching and burning on the skin
  • Red or raised welts
  • Bed bug rash in a localized area
