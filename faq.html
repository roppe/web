Q:What is the difference between replacing tiles and reglazing/refinishing them?

When you replace tiles, you remove the old tiles and replace them with new tiles. Reglazing or refinishing tiles is the process of applying a paint like coating on your existing tiles to give them a new look.

Q:How does reglazing/refinishing work?

Typically, a project starts by cleaning the surfaces that are going to be refinished. After cleaning, the areas are etched by acid so that the product will be able to properly adhere to the surface. The walls, fixtures and floors will be masked off with masking paper and tape for protection, then the primer is applied. Finally, a top coat is applied to the surface and your new space is ready to go!

Q:How long does this take?

This will all depend on the project but typically a tub only takes a couple of hours! If the project is a tub and tile it can take a couple of days. For a more concrete timeline, give me a call!
