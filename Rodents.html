Rodent Control Services in NY

One of the things you must understand about rodents is they are similar to roaches in that they can adapt to their habitats. This is why you will see mice or rats inside of empty homes, inhabited homes, barns, and more. They are able to scavenge for their food and they will eat just about whatever they can get their teeth into. If a rodent runs out of a food source, they will simply find another food source and consume it. They are not limited to just one type of source, which is why they are able to infest homes and spaces very easily.

One reason rodents are hard to control too is because they are often awake and active when humans are asleep. This means that you more than likely miss their activity, so you have no idea they are there.

A female rat is able to produce babies every three weeks and they give birth to up to 10 at a time. This means that in a 9-week period, 30 more rats may be introduced to your home from that ONE female alone. Rat and mouse colonies can grow extremely quickly.
