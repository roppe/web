Bees

If you have a large swarm of bees near your home or you notice an over-sized bee nest, you may be alarmed and worried. It is important that you do not approach the nest or bees and you call a licensed exterminator to help. Bees are crucial to the ecosystem because they assist in pollenating plants and flowers. While you may be amazed by the beauty of the bees and their hive, they can pose a serious danger to your family, especially if the hive becomes disturbed.

One thing to keep in the back of your mind is that bees become angry when their hive is disturbed, which means that they may be aggressive towards you or someone in your home. While bees are generally less aggressive than wasps, they will attack if they feel they or their hive is threatened.

Watch Out for Bee Stings

Bees are, in general, passive by nature and they will not simply attack you because you are present somewhere nearby. However, bees WILL sting you if they feel that their hive is being threatened. It is important to be mindful of a beehive when you notice one and keep your distance from it.

If you have been stung by a bee, you should seek medical attention if you think that you may be allergic or if you suffer from adverse reactions to it.
