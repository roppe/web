The Reglazing Process

We remove the old caulk or old paint from the tub
We repair any chips or worn out areas
We clean and wash the surface of the fixture
We apply a bonding primer and then sand down the surface
We spray a top coat in the color of your choice
Job done – no overspray and no mess

Care & Maintance:

Allow to cure for at least 48 hours before using
When cleaning, do not use harsh chemicals or abrasive materials
Abrasive cleaners can cause damage to the reglazed surface
The use of non-abrasive liquid cleaners is encouraged.
Do NOT use acids or bleach
Ask our experts if you have any questions about your refinished bathtub
