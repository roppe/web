Our Process

Removal of old caulk or old paint from the Tub
Fix any chips or worn areas on the Tub
Clean out the Tub,wash the surfaces
Apply a bonding primer,Sand down the surface
We spray a top coat color of your choice
Guaranteed NO over spray,NO mess

Care & Maintance:

Allow everything to cure for at least 48 hours before using.
When cleaning do not use any harsh chemicals or anything abrasive
Abrasive cleaners can cause small scratches be cerafule
Use only non-abrasive or liquid cleaners.
Do NOT use harsh acids or bleach.
Call us if you have any questions about your refinished bathtub.
